The 21 Studios Experience

Where artistry
meets intention

From the moment you book until your final gallery arrives in your inbox, every detail of your 21 Studios experience is thoughtfully crafted. We believe photography is more than just beautiful images — it's a feeling captured in light and shadow, a story told without words.

01

Book Your Session

The journey begins with a simple booking through our Pixieset platform. Once you select your session type, you'll receive a personalized questionnaire to help us understand your vision, preferred style, and any special requests. We'll then confirm your date, time, and location — indoors at our studio or at a location of your choosing.

Via Pixieset
02

The Photoshoot

On your session day, arrive ready to be yourself. We create a relaxed, unhurried atmosphere so that genuine emotion flows naturally. Whether it's the quiet wonder of a newborn, the laughter of a family reunion, or the electric connection between two people in love — we are ready to capture it all. Sessions typically run 30–60 minutes depending on your package.

In Studio or On Location
03

Editing & Refinement

After your session, we carefully select and hand-edit every image with our signature cinematic touch. Each photo is color-graded, retouched, and refined to ensure it meets our exacting standards. We don't rush this process — great photography deserves the time it takes to be perfect.

48–72 Hour Turnaround
04

Gallery Delivery

Your finished gallery is delivered directly through your private Pixieset gallery link. You'll receive an email with a secure link and, if applicable, a private password. Browse your images in a beautiful online gallery, download in full resolution, and easily share with family and friends. Additional image purchases are available directly through the gallery.
